Rules

Goal: End up with four identical cards!

  • Four cards are dealt to each player and four are placed face-up on the table. Players can pick a card from the ones on the table (the one that suits them best) on the condition that they leave one in its place.
  • The player who gets to choose first is determined clockwise (each time a different player chooses first).
  • After everyone has chosen (a player may choose not to change a card), a «sweep» occurs: the previous cards on the table are collected, and four new cards are dealt face down. The process continues as in the beginning.
  • When a player collects four identical cards, he must say the word "Games" to win.
